Research

Self-cleaning, anti-soiling, and anti-reflective surface coatings are a great way to increase solar panel efficiency. Hydrophobic coatings:

 - Prevent dust from accumulating on the surface.
- Create an evironment where glass can be self cleaned by naturally occuring dew.
- Reduce glass surface reflection to allow more light to pass through.

This research had produced: discovery of the Dust Herding mechanism and proof of concept for natural dew cleaning on hybrid (hydropobic/hydrophilic) surfaces.
You can read more about my research on CUNY Grad Center's Spotlight summary, in my Ph.D. Thesis, or watch me present my research at the CUNY Graduate Center Dissertation Showcase.
