research

During my Ph.D. research I have worked to develop self-cleaning, anti-soiling, and anti-reflective surface coatings for solar panel glass. These coatings prevent dust from accumulating on the surface of the solar panel, lowering the reflection of the sunlight and thus increasing the efficiency of the PV module. Anti-reflective component of the surface coating increases the amount of light that would normally pass through the glass to reach the solar module underneath, futher increasing the efficiency of the PV module.
