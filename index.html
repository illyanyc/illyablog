about me

Welcome to my personal page. I am a registered investment advisor in NY and hold a Ph.D. in chemistry from City University of New York.

I have researched superhydrophobic nano-coatings for anti-soiling and self-cleaning solar glass applications as well as anti-reflective coatings. These coatings help make solar panels more efficeint by stopping dust from accumulating on their surface. Anti-reflective coatings help solar panels get more light. Currently, I am the CEO, CIO and founder of Alpha Inertia Capital, which is a quant investment advisory firm powered by machine learning and big data.

Currently I am interested in: machine learning models trained on backtest data (LSTM on TF), blockchain developement (Solidity), and automated trade excecution using IBKR platform.

I'm proficient in python and C#, and am quite nimble with Numpy/pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, OpenCV, Matplotlib/ Plotly/ hvPlot and Google Cloud Platform. I'm in terested in perfecting my Amazon AWS and Solidity skills. I'm an avid user of jupyter notebooks for developement and GCP deployments for production.

I like to travel and read. You can check out my IG for some of the most recent travel pics, and my reading list.
